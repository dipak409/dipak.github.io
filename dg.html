Defence Guardian
Details

Title: Defence Guardian

Platform: PC (Windows OS x64, x86)

Genre: strategy game

Target: Casual gamer (aging from 12 - 30)

Developed In: Unity game engine

YouTube Video

Tower defense games understand a tower as an object, fixed in place, that is used to shoot at enemies to stop them from reaching an objective point.

Towers can also perform non-attack effects, like slowing enemies down, or generating more currency for a player to buy even more towers.

The player will often have the option to upgrade towers to higher levels to make them more powerful or have a longer range.

AR Ballon Blaster
Details

Title: AR Balloon Blaster

Platform: Android

Target: Casual gamer

Developed: Unity Technologies

AR Version and Name: AR foundation (2.1.8)

YouTube Video

After clicking on the play button, you have to wait for 3 seconds then Balloon pops up on your screen.

If you shoot that balloon your score will automatically increase by two.

If you do not shoot the balloon after going to few heights it will automatically destroy and your score will be deducted by one.
